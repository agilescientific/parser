About geotherm.ai

What? This finds geological information from plaintext. Currently it just finds geochronological intervals and gives details about them, but will hopefully be extended.

Whence? This was hacked together by Agile Scientific

Examples The results are from the International Commission on Stratigraphy's geologic time scale. Here are some things this web app might do one day:

  1. Recognition of locally-used intervals - we need sources for these!
  2. Geolocation of places mentioned, possibly including of geologically interesting locations (that is, recognition of geological places such as the Parana Basin and such).
  3. Recognition of more types of geological things, not just intervals.
    • Rock types!
    • Mineral names!
    • Lithostratigraphic things!
